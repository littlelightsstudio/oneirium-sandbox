◆ Transition Pair Reference ◆

A transition completes when a card with the From tag and a card with the To tag are both on your arc with at least one card between them. Each completed transition scores +3 CP and triggers its Echo effect. Each tag enables only one transition at a time.

Tag families: Tension Resolve Warmth Motion Stillness All red tags start transitions · grey tags are descriptive
